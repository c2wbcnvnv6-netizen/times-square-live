How to monitor for incidents (riots / unrest)

  • Watch live feeds for: sudden large police formations, people running, smoke, broken glass, barriers being erected, or unusual dispersal of crowds.
  • Cross-reference multiple angles simultaneously (use this page + EarthCam site).
  • Listen to audio where available (EarthCam street cam often has sound).
  • Check recent X posts with geo / keyword filters for eyewitness video (search "Times Square" + riot terms or "Brazil" + incident).
  • Official channels: @NYPD, @NYPDMidtown, local news (FOX5, ABC7NY, NY1) for statements.
  • Note: Large festive crowds (e.g. soccer fans) create high density, loud noise, and require police for traffic/crowd control. This is normal and not a riot indicator.
